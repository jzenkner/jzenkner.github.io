Janis Zenkner

PhD Student | Program Synthesis | AI & Machine Learning

I am a PhD student specializing in program synthesis and machine learning. My work focuses on developing neural architectures for task decomposition and automated problem-solving.

I earned my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Medical Engineering from Friedrich-Alexander University Erlangen-Nuremberg. I began my PhD at the University of Mannheim, where I focused on AI-driven program synthesis. When my doctoral advisor moved to TU Clausthal, I continued my research there in the Machine Learning and Cognitive Software group. My work aims to advance AI-driven automation and intelligent systems through efficient program synthesis techniques.
